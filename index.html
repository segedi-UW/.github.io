How I got here

I spent my first year and a half of college thinking I was going to get a biology or chemistry degree. During that pursuit I ended up spending a good chunk of my time creating programs to complete homework and learning about programming to do so. I realized within the year what I really wanted to pursue, quickly adapting to computer science. I continued to learn outside of the classroom, doing online coursework such as the Odin Project in addition to my university studies. Since I did very well for myself in highschool, I was able to complete a bachelors in three years instead of 4.

Who I am

I consider myself a team player, and I have not yet met someone I could not work with. I have worked with Roofers to medical professionals to computer science majors and have been successful with all.
